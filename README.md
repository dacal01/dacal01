## 🔬 About me

I'm a **PhD Candidate at the University of Granada**, researching language models and conversational AI applied to **mental health**. My work sits at the intersection of NLP, speech analytics, and clinical applications — in collaboration with international consortia like **CRYSTAL** and **MENHIR**.

Alongside my research, I work as an **ML Engineer at [MDHub](https://mdhub.ai) (YC S24, San Francisco)**, where I design and ship end-to-end AI products: from RAG pipelines and LLM integrations to Vue.js frontends and Node.js backends.

Previously, I led the development of **fake voice detection models** at the RTVE-UGR Chair on Deep Speech Synthesis — models now used in production by RTVE and IVERES teams.
